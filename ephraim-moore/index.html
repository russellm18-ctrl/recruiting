About

Ephraim is a Class of 2027 distance runner representing Olympia High School, competing across the 800m, 1600m, and Cross Country events.

Entering his senior year, Ephraim is executing a highly focused off-season training block to secure a varsity roster spot. He brings a gritty, work-first mentality to the track—a trait that directly mirrors his upward academic trajectory and disciplined approach in the classroom. He is currently exploring collegiate programs with strong foundations in sports medicine and environmental studies.
