06

Questions

Will a coach actually find this page?

Each profile is built with structured data and hosted on an established domain, so it's designed to surface when a coach searches your athlete's name. Just as importantly, it gives you one clean, professional link to drop directly into recruiting emails.

My athlete is only a freshman or sophomore — too early?

No. Starting earlier means the page builds search authority over time and you control the story from the start. Annual updates keep PRs and film current as your athlete develops.

How is this different from a national recruiting service?

Those are databases where your athlete is one of thousands. This is a single, ownable, search-optimized page with real race photography and film, built by a credentialed photographer who's actually at your meets.

Do you have to take new photos?

Not for the base profile — it uses existing meet coverage where available. A dedicated action and portrait shoot is the upgrade if you want fresh imagery made for the page.

Who owns the page?

By default it's hosted on my domain so it benefits from established search authority. The All-American tier (or the add-on) gives your athlete their own custom domain to own permanently.
