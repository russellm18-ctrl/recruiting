ABOUT OLIVIA

Olivia Decker is a Class of 2029 distance runner from Shoreline, Washington, racing for Shorewood High School and training year-round with the Bryant Bandits club program. Across her freshman season she moved through the 1600m, 3200m, and 5000m cross country distances with a steady drop in times, highlighted by a personal best in the 3200m at the WIAA 3A State Championships and a 16-second PR at the Nike Cross Country Northwest Regional in Spokane.

Running under the Shorewood banner alongside teammates Violet Koslowsky and Cleo Dalasta, Decker has built a reputation for closing hard in championship-tier races — Birger Solberg, Oregon Relays, and NXR Northwest among them. Academically she carries a 4.0 GPA and intends to pursue pre-med. Coaches Paul Villanueva and Joel Reese lead the Shorewood program; Matt Koenigs coaches her club training with Bryant Bandits.
